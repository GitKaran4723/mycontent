## **1\. What is an Experiment?**

* In everyday life, the word *experiment* means "to try something and see what happens."

* In **probability**, an **experiment (or random experiment)** is an action or process that:

  1. Can be repeated under the same conditions.

  2. Has two or more possible outcomes.

  3. The outcome cannot be predicted with certainty **before** it happens.
